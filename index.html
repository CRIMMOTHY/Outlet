About The Club

Welcome to The Outlet, our school's student-run newspaper club! We're dedicated to creating a space where students can share their voices, celebrate our community, and discover stories that inspire and entertain.

Whether it's uncovering the latest campus news, highlighting student achievements, or sparking conversations about topics that matter, The Outlet gives every student a platform to be heard. Our goal is to connect, inform, and inspire through creativity, collaboration, and storytelling.
